IDLE
Keys are used only in your browser to read Bybit positions and executions.
Default logic: select an open position, press Calculate, and the app uses the current market price as the default profit threshold. Move the slider to test a custom entry layer.
Price threshold—
LONG: counts Open Long entries at or below the effective threshold. SHORT: counts Open Short entries at or above the effective threshold. Effective threshold is capped by current price so non-profitable entries are not counted. Closings are subtracted using the verified CreateByClosing / closedSize logic.
